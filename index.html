What I do

Customer Support

I got hooked on Customer Support when I worked for Travelocity back in 1999. Since then I've used my empathy, talent for technical things, and enthusiasm for helping people to create positive customer experiences — by treating people with respect, digging deep into problems to find solutions, and not giving up until they're solved. I believe everyone is just trying to do their best, and sometimes they just need a friendly, confident person to help them do that.

Tech Support

I have a knack for understanding and fixing technical things, so I really enjoy the Tech Support field. I decided to start enhancing my technical skills by studying things like Routing and Switching and Full-Stack Development. I prefer asking, "How can I do that?" rather than "Can I do that?" Regardless of what I’ve learned, I’ve always found it fascinating, and I love using my ability to help people and build things.

Development

Writing code is a new love of mine. I have worked with Wordpress before and had good results, but my favorite thing to do is learn how to build things the way I want them to look, from scratch. I have worked with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and a little bit of Java, among other things, and I used the first three to build this website. I am a lifetime learner, and I look forward to seeing where this path will take me.

My Work

Who I am

Customer Support Pro & Developer based out of Missouri

Born in a small town in Missouri, USA, I grew up understanding the importance of treating all people with respect by default. This knowledge has served me well in everything I have done — from being a waitress, to working in Home Health, to Customer Support & Billing, to Technical Support, to working in Human Resources — people respond favorably when you treat them with respect and make them feel heard.

I have found that I feel the most fulfilled when I combine my caring for people with my love for solving problems. As a mom who is also a Customer Service Professional that has received the highest CSAT scores my manager had ever seen, taking ownership of problems and presenting things in a caring way comes naturally. I am a lifetime learner with a high standard for excellence — and my development projects and the results I have gotten at my previous jobs are proof.

You're invited to look around and check out my projects and social links on this website, and please feel free to contact me via the contact form, or directly at my contact details listed in the contact section. I will continue to add projects to my portfolio as I create them, so I would love it if you were to check back to see if I've added more. I would love to discuss how my experience can help you achieve your goals, and I look forward to hearing from you.
